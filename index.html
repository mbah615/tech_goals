Introduction



My name is John Mbah Yashim. I am a final year student majoring in Computer Science at the American University of Nigeria (AUN), Yola. I am a dedicated, enthusiastic, and energetic individual that is open minded, eager to learn new things, and aims at adding value wherever I go. As an individual who aspires to grow myself in the tech field.

Currently, I have set certain objectives which I aim to achieve within a two-year span. Personally, I call these fixed objectives my “5-Point Agenda.” They are meant to serve as a guide to keep me focused and committed.

I aim to be a certified Frontend Developer who is conversant with HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React JS by completing online or in-person courses from platforms like Udemy and Freecodecamp, and developing 2 projects using these technologies
I aim to build a network of friends and build lifelong relationships with like-minded indivuals that are involved in tech field by participating in tech-related internships such as HNG, attending tech-related conferences, and contributing to projects and answering questions on online communities like Stack Overflow and GitHub
I aim to identify and choose a mentor in the tech field whose advice, character, and commitment to the tech-globe inspires me to be the best I can be
I aim to build my critical thinking and problem-solving skills in Python by taking part in online challenges that focus on aspects that I am weak in such as recursion.
I aim to make a difference in my hometown by helping the local community children that have an interest in venturing into tech by scheduling physical classes and tutorials through partnerships with secondary and primary schools
